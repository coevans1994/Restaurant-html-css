## User Stories

Pattern:

...
AS A/AN _____ (Who?)
I Want to _____ (What?)
So That _____ (Why?)
...

Examples:

...
As a customer
I want to dine at a restaurant
with a good steak.

As an employee
I want to work at a
fine dining restaurant.

As a food lover
I want to eat at
a restaurant with a good
variety of food.
...# Restaurant-html-css
